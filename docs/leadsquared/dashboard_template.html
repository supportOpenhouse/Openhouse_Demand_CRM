Cohort Retention — % of activated brokers (≥1 visit ever)

Size = brokers in the cohort who ever did ≥1 visit (not all onboarded). Each cell = % of those brokers active that month — true retention of the brokers who actually started.

Cohort Retention — rolling 3-month active % (of activated brokers)

Each cell = % of the cohort's activated brokers active in that month OR either of the 2 prior months (e.g. May = active in Mar, Apr or May). Smooths single-month gaps — a broker on a 2-month cadence still counts as retained.
